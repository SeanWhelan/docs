## Welcome to Estuary Flow

Estuary Flow is a unified data movement and transformation platform that synchronizes your systems – SaaS, databases, streaming, and more – around the same datasets, updating in milliseconds. Flow combines the easy cross-system integration of an ELT tool with a flexible streaming backbone.
